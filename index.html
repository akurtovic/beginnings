Start your hunt for fonts with these

beginnings

Beginnings is devoted to identifying complimentary font combinations in the Google web fonts directory. Each section is composed of the title and opening paragraphs of some of my favorite books, set in a mix of fonts that work well together. Hover over each title or paragraph to get the name of the font, or click to go directly to the Google fonts page. And if you don't fall in love with any fonts, perhaps you will discover an interesting book.
